AI Generated (Human Reviewed)

This label indicates that AI generated most or all of the content, but a human reviewed, corrected and approved it before publication. Human oversight ensures factual accuracy and appropriate tone.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • The main text was generated by AI and then reviewed by a human before publication.
  • The published version includes human edits, corrections or approvals.

Don'ts

  • Does not guarantee the content is error-free after review.
  • Does not imply an external certification or audit was performed.
  • Does not mean the review replaced subject-matter expert verification where needed.

Examples

  • OK: AI writes a blog article which is then edited and approved by a human editor.
  • OK: AI generates product descriptions which are human-verified before publishing.
  • Not OK: Fully automated publishing — use “AI Generated (Automated)”.
