AI Illustration

This label indicates that AI was used to generate or enhance visuals (images, illustrations, icons, textures) while the written content itself remains fully human-created and unaltered.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Some or all visuals on this page were generated or enhanced with AI.
  • Text on this page was written or approved by people and is not AI-authored.
  • Captions or alt text may indicate which images were AI-created.

Don'ts

  • Does not mean the visuals are fact-checked or authoritative.
  • Does not mean AI influenced the article’s claims or conclusions.
  • Does not assert image licensing or legal rights — check credits where provided.

Examples

  • OK: A human-written article illustrated with AI-generated header images.
  • OK: A blog post with an AI-generated diagram or icon.
  • Not OK: Text partially drafted by AI — use “AI Support” instead.
