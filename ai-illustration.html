AI Illustration

This label indicates that AI was used to generate or enhance visuals (images, illustrations, icons, textures) while the written content itself remains fully human-created and unaltered.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Use when AI-generated imagery is included but the text content is fully human-written.
  • Ensure that AI visuals do not alter the meaning of the content.
  • Provide alt text that describes the image's purpose, not the AI model used.

Don'ts

  • Don’t use this label if AI contributed to the writing or translation of the text.
  • Don’t apply if the visuals significantly shape the meaning or claims of the content.
  • Don’t pair with contradictory labels like “AI Generated (Automated)”.

Examples

  • OK: A human-written article illustrated with AI-generated header images.
  • OK: A blog post with an AI-generated diagram or icon.
  • Not OK: Text partially drafted by AI — use “AI Support” instead.
