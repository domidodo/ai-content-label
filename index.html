Questions & Answers

Is the AI Content Label set free to use?

Yes. All labels are completely free to use without any conditions. You may include them on personal, commercial, or nonprofit websites.

Do I need to provide attribution?

No attribution is required. However, linking to the project website is appreciated so users can learn what the labels mean.

Can I modify the labels?

You may resize or optimize the files, but the visual meaning of the labels should remain intact to avoid confusing users.

Are the labels available as SVG files?

Yes. All labels are provided as scalable SVG files for maximum clarity on all devices.

Do the labels require JavaScript?

No. The labels are simple image files. You can add them anywhere on your page with a standard <img> element.

How should I choose the correct label?

Always select the highest applicable level of AI involvement. For example: Automated > Reviewed > Support > Translation.

Where should I place the label on my website?

For best visibility and consistency, we recommend placing the label in the header or footer of your page. Either location ensures users can quickly understand the level of AI involvement without disrupting the main content.

Can I use the labels in printed material or videos?

Yes. The labels may also be used in offline media, presentations, videos, or documentation.
