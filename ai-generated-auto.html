AI Generated (Automated)

This label indicates that content was created, updated or published fully automatically by AI systems without human intervention, oversight or manual review.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Use for content that is fully automated from generation to publication.
  • Ensure systems include monitoring to avoid harmful or misleading outputs.
  • Disclose automation clearly to maintain transparency with users.

Don'ts

  • Don’t use this label if a human reviews or edits the AI output.
  • Don’t combine with “AI Support” or “Reviewed” labels.
  • Don’t use for sensitive content such as medical, financial or legal information.

Examples

  • OK: Automated daily weather summaries generated and published by AI.
  • OK: AI-generated stock market overviews in real time.
  • Not OK: AI output checked by an editor — use “AI Generated (Human Reviewed)”.
