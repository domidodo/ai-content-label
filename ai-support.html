AI Support

This label indicates that a human wrote the content, but AI assisted in tasks such as editing, ideation, restructuring, summarizing, or improving clarity — without independently generating the final text.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • AI provided editorial assistance such as phrasing, grammar or idea suggestions.
  • A human author reviewed and finalized the content.
  • The label indicates the presence of AI-assisted editorial steps.

Don'ts

  • Does not mean the content was primarily written by AI.
  • Does not indicate any formal external audit of the editorial process.
  • Does not mean every AI suggestion was accepted unchanged.

Examples

  • OK: A human drafts an article and uses AI to improve grammar and clarity.
  • OK: AI suggests bullet points or ideas incorporated manually.
  • Not OK: AI writes an entire section — use “AI Generated (Human Reviewed)” instead.
