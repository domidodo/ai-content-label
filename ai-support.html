AI Support

This label indicates that a human wrote the content, but AI assisted in tasks such as editing, ideation, restructuring, summarizing, or improving clarity — without independently generating the final text.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Use when AI tools support human writing but do not author the content.
  • Be transparent about the nature of AI involvement.
  • Review AI suggestions critically before integrating them.

Don'ts

  • Don’t use this label if whole sections were generated by AI.
  • Don’t apply if content was auto-generated with minimal human work.
  • Don’t misrepresent AI-generated passages as merely “assisted”.

Examples

  • OK: A human drafts an article and uses AI to improve grammar and clarity.
  • OK: AI suggests bullet points or ideas incorporated manually.
  • Not OK: AI writes an entire section — use “AI Generated (Human Reviewed)” instead.
