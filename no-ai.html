No AI

This label indicates that no artificial intelligence was used in the creation, editing or production of this content. Text, images and metadata were created and reviewed by humans only.

Dos and Don'ts

Dos

  • Use only when no AI tools (translation, autocomplete, image tools) were involved.
  • Place the badge where visitors can easily find it (header or footer recommended).
  • Keep an internal audit note documenting that the content was human-only.

Don'ts

  • Do not apply this label if any AI-assisted drafting, editing or image generation took place.
  • Avoid using it for marketing if it's not factually correct.
  • Do not combine with statements that contradict the label (e.g. "No AI" + "auto translated").

Examples

  • OK: A feature article written and copy-edited by a journalist and editor without any AI tools.
  • Not OK: An article drafted with AI autocomplete — do not use "No AI".
  • OK: Original photographs shot by a photographer and not altered by generative tools.
